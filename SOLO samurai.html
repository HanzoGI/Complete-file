Solo Samurai

The Path of the Masterless Ronin

Japan, 1603. The era of war draws to a fragile close, leaving thousands of warriors without a master. You are one of them—a solitary ronin condemned to wander the shifting landscapes of a deeply fractured nation.

From the snow-swept northern mountain peaks of Hokkaido down to the treacherous blood-stained bamboo thickets of Kyoto, your blade tracks a path of survival, vengeance, and hidden redemption. No lord dictates your movements, and no army guards your flank.

Every step through the vast provinces brings deadly encounters with rival swordsmen, corrupt clan magistrates, and shadowy mythical threats. In this isolated pilgrimage across the feudal empire, your edge is your only law, and your focus is your only shield.
