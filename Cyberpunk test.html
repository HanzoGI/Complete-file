The Solitary Blade

Lone Survivor of the Cyber Grid

Neo-Kyoto, 2099. The mega-corporations executed the Global Purge, dismantling the ancient military factions and short-circuiting every honorable digital consciousness. The great Cyber Samurai Order was systematically deleted overnight—except for one.

You are that anomaly. A lone wandering ghost in a shell of carbon-fiber plates and overclocked neural processors, navigating a hyper-connected wasteland where chrome is currency and survival requires absolute silence.

With a high-frequency thermal katana slung across your back and an offline memory core keeping your history safe, you stalk the rain-drenched neon alleys alone. You hold the last preserved combat protocols of a dead era, operating as the final shield against a completely lawless electronic empire.
